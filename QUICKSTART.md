## Daily Routine

### Sustainable Learning Schedule

**Weekdays** (1.5-2 hours):
- 1 hour: Bootcamp lesson
- 30 minutes: Hands-on exercises
- 15 minutes: Notes and reflection

**Weekends** (2-3 hours):
- 1 hour: Bootcamp lesson
- 1 hour: Extra practice or project work
- 30 minutes: Review week's learning

### Tips for Success

1. **Same time every day** - Build a habit
2. **Eliminate distractions** - Phone away, focus mode
3. **Take notes** - Write down key concepts
4. **Code along** - Don't just read, type the code
5. **Ask questions** - Use communities when stuck

---

## Common First Week Issues

### "I'm stuck on Day X"

**Solutions**:
- Re-read the instructions carefully
- Search the error message online
- Skip and come back tomorrow
- Ask in community forums
- Check the bootcamp's issues/discussions

### "This is taking longer than 1 hour"

**That's normal!** Estimates are for experienced learners. Taking 1.5-2 hours per day is fine, especially at the start.

### "I don't understand a concept"

**Try this**:
1. Re-read the explanation
2. Search for the concept on YouTube
3. Try the exercises anyway (learning by doing)
4. Ask in communities
5. Move forward, it may click later

### "I missed a day"

**No problem!** Just continue where you left off. Consistency matters more than perfection.
