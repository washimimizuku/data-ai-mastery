# Prerequisites: Unity Catalog Governance Project

## Required Bootcamps

### 30 Days of Databricks for Data and AI
**Mandatory Lessons:**
- Day 1: Databricks Architecture & Workspace Setup
- Day 2: Clusters & Compute Management
- Day 3: Notebooks & Collaborative Development
- Day 4: Unity Catalog Fundamentals
- Day 5: Metastore & Catalog Management
- Day 6: Data Governance & Lineage
- Day 7: Access Control & Permissions
- Day 8: Data Classification & Tagging
- Day 9: Audit Logging & Compliance
- Day 15: Security Best Practices
- Day 16: Identity & Access Management
- Day 25: Multi-Workspace Governance

**Nice to Have:**
- Day 10: Delta Lake Fundamentals
- Day 11: Data Sharing & Collaboration
- Day 17: Cost Management & Optimization
- Day 18: Performance Monitoring

### 30 Days of SQL for Data and AI
**Mandatory Lessons:**
- Day 1-5: SQL Fundamentals (SELECT, WHERE, JOIN)
- Day 6-10: Advanced Queries (Subqueries, CTEs, Window Functions)
- Day 15-17: Data Security & Access Control
- Day 20-22: Performance Optimization

**Nice to Have:**
- Day 11-14: Data Manipulation (INSERT, UPDATE, DELETE)
- Day 18-19: Stored Procedures & Functions

### 100 Days of Data and AI
**Mandatory Lessons:**
- Day 81-85: Data Governance Fundamentals
- Day 86-90: Data Lineage & Cataloging
- Day 91-95: Compliance & Auditing

**Nice to Have:**
- Day 76-80: Monitoring & Observability
- Day 96-100: Advanced Governance Topics

## Estimated Prerequisites Time
- **Mandatory**: 40-50 hours
- **Nice to Have**: 15-20 hours
- **Total Recommended**: 55-70 hours

## Skills Validation
Before starting this project, ensure you can:
- Navigate Databricks workspace and Unity Catalog
- Configure metastores and manage catalogs
- Implement fine-grained access controls
- Set up data classification and tagging policies
- Monitor data lineage and usage patterns
- Configure audit logging and compliance reporting
