- Day 1-20: Python & Data Processing Fundamentals

- Day 31-40: Real-Time Processing & Data Quality
- Day 41-50: Pipeline Orchestration & Architecture
- Day 51-70: Machine Learning & Deep Learning
- Day 71-85: NLP & Large Language Models
- Day 86-100: Advanced ML & LLM Topics

### 60 Days of Advanced Data and AI

- Day 51-60: Advanced Analytics & AI Systems

### 30 Days of SQL for Data and AI
**Mandatory Lessons:**

### 30 Days of Python for Data and AI
**Mandatory Lessons:**

**Nice to Have:**
### 30 Days of Rust for Data and AI

## Estimated Prerequisites Time
- **Mandatory**: 140-160 hours
- **Nice to Have**: 30-35 hours
- **Total Recommended**: 170-195 hours

## Skills Validation
Before starting this project, ensure you can:
- Design and implement end-to-end data and AI platforms
- Build scalable data pipelines and real-time processing systems
- Deploy and manage ML/AI models in production
- Implement advanced analytics and visualization systems
- Handle multi-tenant architecture and security
- Monitor and optimize platform performance across all components
